🔔 Issue 2 — Spike Detection Has Too Much Noise

Code fix needed
269 noise spikes across three suppliers — threshold is too low

The current spike rule fires when 7D demand is 1.5× the 30D rate AND 7D ≥ 2 units. The result: an item that sold 2 units this week vs 2 units all month triggers a spike (4.3× ratio). That's mathematically correct but practically meaningless — it's just timing noise on slow movers. At 2 units in 7 days, you can't tell if demand is accelerating or if two people happened to buy the same thing on the same day.
